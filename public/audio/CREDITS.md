All samples are CC0 (public-domain dedication) — no attribution required; listed for provenance.

Each file is a seamless ~42 s loop: a 45 s segment with its 3 s tail crossfaded into its
head (ffmpeg `acrossfade`), so `loop: true` wraps without a seam.

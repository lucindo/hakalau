# Audio credits

All samples are public-domain (CC0/PD Mark) or first-party recordings — no attribution
required; listed for provenance.

Each nature file is a seamless loop: its 3 s tail crossfaded into its head (ffmpeg
`acrossfade`), so `loop: true` wraps without a seam. The bell is authored as a loop and
ships unmodified.
